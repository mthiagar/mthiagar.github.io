About Me

Professionaly I'm a Medical Laboratory Technologist working in molecular genetics. In my spare time I like to play with web development, and build data science projects using what ever data I can get my hands on. This site is one example, but check out my portfolio for more examples of things I've done. In more recent years, I've taken an interest in the wider health system, and have completed a Master of Health Informatics to get some more formal exposure to the skills needed to address informatics and IT challenges faced in healthcare. However, not everything I do is related to healthcare. In general, I like to work with whatever data I can get my hands on. This site, and my portfolio are still a work in progress!
